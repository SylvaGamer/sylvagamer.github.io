Colored Hex Blocks

Minecraft Mod

Colored Hex Blocks is a mod meant to solve the issue of their not being enough colors for flat blocks in Minecraft. So I decided to implement the dying process from leather armor and put it onto a custom modded flat block, adding stairs, slabs, walls, etc. I even went so far to give it glass and glass pane variants
